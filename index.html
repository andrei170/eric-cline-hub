Load-Bearing Infrastructure
01

No Unified Hub Connecting The Scattered Funnel

theericcline.com is a navigation shell. The actual offers, the $997 course, GOAT Gang, ELITE, the A-Z Bootcamp, all live as separate standalone pages on the go.theericcline.com subdomain. There is no single front door. A cold visitor, someone who heard you on REFUSE TO LOSE, got referred by a GOAT Gang member, found a clip, lands on whichever single funnel page the link happens to point at. They see one offer, out of context, with no map of the rest. Every other fix on this list needs a destination, and right now there isn't one.

Cost of Waiting

~$3-9K/mo in course sales and coaching applications that never happen because cold traffic lands mid-funnel with no context and bounces. Across 12 months, ~$36K-$108K leaking, and it compounds, because every month of audience growth arrives into the same disconnected scatter.

6 months from now ·~$18-54K never collected. Plus six months of podcast episodes, YouTube uploads, and IG growth all pointing at a funnel with no front door.

Fix · One standalone audience hub, hero VSL, free lead magnet opt-in above the fold, operator-story credibility section, the full offer ladder made legible, real-testimonial receipts block, FAQ, wholesaling compliance disclaimer footer. Less than 7 days to deploy. Year-1 floor $36K-$108K direct, target $75K-$220K with full execution. Load-bearing for #2, #3, #4, #5.
Highest Compounding Floor
02

No Email-Gated Free Lead Magnet Building An Owned List

Every funnel page I fetched was a paid sales page or a checkout. The one free-adjacent asset, a "Script Download," appears to be an ungated direct download, not an email-gated list-builder. A ~110K Instagram audience, plus YouTube, TikTok, and a podcast, and there is no observable free asset converting a follower into an email subscriber you own. The email list is the only audience asset no algorithm can take away, and right now it is barely being built. You already have the material that makes a great lead magnet sitting in your paid products, the cold-call script, the 5-step process.

Cost of Waiting

A free lead magnet promoted across IG, YouTube, TikTok, and the podcast could conservatively add thousands of email subscribers in Year-1. At a conservative downstream conversion into the existing ladder, that list is worth ~$40K-$110K Year-1 that is currently not being built at all.

6 months from now ·~$20-55K of list value never built, plus the nurture sequence that would have warmed those subscribers toward the course and GOAT Gang never ran.

Fix · One flagship lead magnet, a free First-Call Script + 5-Step Sales Process cheat sheet, email-gated on the hub, backed by a 5-email nurture sequence. Promoted from every channel's bio, descriptions, and show notes. Year-1 floor $40K-$110K, target $90K-$240K with full execution, compounding.
Conversion Architecture
03

No Legible Mid-Tier Step In The Ladder

The ladder, as a cold visitor experiences it, runs: $997 DIY course, then GOAT Gang with the price hidden behind a checkout click, then ELITE with the price hidden behind a checkout click. A buyer who finishes the $997 course and is ready for more hits a wall. They cannot self-qualify, cannot see where they fit, cannot picture the path. Hidden pricing is a normal tactic for the top tier, but when every tier above the entry course hides its commitment level, the ladder stops working as a ladder. The fix is not to publish every price, it is to make the ladder legible so the buyer can place themselves on it.

Cost of Waiting

Course buyers who would have climbed to GOAT Gang or ELITE but could not see the path, conservatively ~$2.5-7K/mo in mid-tier and high-tier conversions that stall at the opaque step. Across 12 months, ~$30K-$84K leaking because the ladder is not legible.

6 months from now ·~$15-42K never collected, plus every course buyer in that window who finished, was ready, and quietly drifted because they couldn't see where to go next.

Fix · A legible ladder section on the hub and landing page, every tier named, a one-line who-it's-for, a plain-language commitment description, a clear next step. Tier shape hinted, exact coaching prices stay hinted not dropped. Year-1 floor $30K-$84K direct, target $60K-$170K with full execution.
Warm-Audience Capture · Compounding
04

No Nurture Funnel For The REFUSE TO LOSE Podcast Audience

You host the REFUSE TO LOSE podcast, an active asset with deal-story episodes. No funnel was surfaced routing podcast listeners anywhere, not into a free asset, not into the email list, not into the paid ladder. Podcast listeners are the warmest, highest-context segment of any creator's audience. They have chosen to spend 30-to-60-minute blocks with you, in your voice, hearing your deal stories in depth. They are pre-sold on the operator. And right now there is no path from "finished the episode" to "joined the list" to "bought the course." The warmest segment in the stack is currently unmonetized as a funnel.

Cost of Waiting

A podcast-to-funnel route, a consistent show-notes CTA, a dedicated listener opt-in, a segmented welcome sequence, conservatively captures ~$2.5-7K/mo in course and GOAT Gang conversions from listeners currently hitting a dead end. Across 12 months, ~$30K-$84K leaking while the podcast keeps publishing.

6 months from now ·~$15-42K never collected, plus every episode published in that window that warmed a listener and then sent them nowhere.

Fix · A podcast nurture funnel, consistent show-notes and in-episode CTA into the free lead magnet, a dedicated podcast-listener opt-in path on the hub, a short segmented welcome sequence. Built on the hub and email infrastructure from #1 and #2. Year-1 floor $30K-$84K, target $60K-$170K with full execution, compounding.
Recurring Engagement · Distribution Compound
05

No LinkedIn Distribution For The Investor-Curious / Aspiring-Wholesaler ICP

Your distribution is concentrated on Instagram, with YouTube, TikTok, and the podcast as secondary channels. A real slice of your ideal buyer does not make buying decisions on Instagram. The aspiring wholesaler weighing a five-figure coaching commitment, the W-2 professional researching whether virtual wholesaling is real before they spend money, the investor-curious operator doing due diligence, those people research on LinkedIn. An operator with your track record is exactly the profile LinkedIn rewards, and that credibility is currently invisible on the one channel where the highest-intent slice of your ICP is screening before they reach out.

Cost of Waiting

LinkedIn (grown over 12 months, posting 3-4x/week): ~$40-120K/yr in course sales, coaching applications, and DealCon tickets never captured because that ICP cannot find you where they search. Pre-call trust that does not compound is a conversion penalty.

12 months from now ·$40-120K+ in compounding distribution never collected. Every month delayed is a month of audience growth that doesn't happen on the channel where the aspiring-wholesaler ICP does its due diligence.

Fix · Monthly recurring engagement. LinkedIn 3 to 4x/week, ghostwritten in your operator-direct voice from existing knowledge, virtual-wholesaling deal breakdowns, the 5-step process taught in public, the operator story told in pieces, "is wholesaling worth it" myth-busting for the due-diligence searcher. All from IP that already exists. Year-1 floor $40K-$120K, target $95K-$270K with full execution, on top of the Install bottlenecks. Combined Install + Recurring Engagement Year-1 floor $180K-$470K, target $380K-$1.05M with full execution, at your audience tier.
