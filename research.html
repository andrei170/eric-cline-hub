8. Compliance + Niche Watch

Real estate wholesaling is a regulated, FTC-watched niche on two axes. The landing page MUST carry a visible compliance disclaimer footer (per A5). Scope:

  • Income claims: results are not typical; outcomes depend on individual effort, market conditions, deal flow, and execution; past results (including Eric's own) do not guarantee future outcomes.
  • Wholesaling legality varies by state. Real estate wholesaling is regulated and restricted in several US states, and some states require a real estate license to wholesale or to perform certain wholesaling activities. Anyone considering wholesaling must check the rules in their own state.
  • Educator, not licensed advisor. Eric Cline is a sales coach and educator, not a licensed real estate broker advising on your specific transaction, not an attorney, not a financial or investment advisor. His content is educational.
  • Consult professionals. Anyone acting on the material should consult licensed professionals and a real estate attorney for state-specific rules before wholesaling.

Do not write guarantee / refund language into any tier copy unless Eric confirms it on Day 1. - Keep all "you can do this" copy outcome-honest: the framework is teachable, the result is not promised.
