The Five Layers (mapped to the five bottlenecks)

Layer 1 · The Hub (Bottleneck #1)

One standalone audience hub at a clean URL. Sections: - Hero with a short VSL (~60-90s preview) and the free lead magnet opt-in above the fold - About + operator-story credibility block (the $250M / $4.5M-exit story, attributed to Eric) - The legible offer ladder (Layer 3) - Real-testimonial receipts block (Jason Corley + Danny Sanchez, confirmed Day 1, otherwise placeholder) - FAQ - A5 wholesaling compliance disclaimer footer

The IG bio link, YouTube channel link, podcast show notes, and LinkedIn profile link all re-point here. Ships in less than 7 days.

Layer 2 · The Free Lead Magnet + Nurture (Bottleneck #2)

One flagship lead magnet, the "Virtual Wholesaler's First-Call Script + 5-Step Sales Process Cheat Sheet" (working title, locked with Eric Day 1). Professionally designed, email-gated on the hub. Backed by the 5-email nurture sequence in email-sequence, which warms the subscriber from the free script toward the $997 course and GOAT Gang.

This is the asset that turns the ~110K rented audience into an owned list. Promoted from every channel's bio / description / show notes.

Layer 3 · The Legible Ladder (Bottleneck #3)

One ladder section, on the hub and the landing page, that makes the existing stack legible:

Tier What it is Who it is for Commitment (hint, not drop)
Free Script The first-call script + 5-step cheat sheet Anyone curious about virtual wholesaling Free, email opt-in
The Course The $997 DIY 7-Figure Wholesaler Sales Program The self-paced operator who wants the full system Accessible one-time, self-paced
GOAT Gang The Skool community + bi-monthly live coaching The operator who wants the room and live access to Eric Recurring membership tier
ELITE The premium A-Z live-coaching blueprint The operator going all-in on a live-coached build Premium application tier
DealCon The live event The operator who wants the room in person Event ticket / included at higher tiers

The exact GOAT Gang and ELITE prices stay hinted, not dropped, per the D100 hint-don't-drop standard. The point is legibility, the buyer can see the whole climb and place themselves on it without a checkout-page click.

Layer 4 · The Podcast Funnel (Bottleneck #4)

A route from REFUSE TO LOSE into the system: - A consistent show-notes and in-episode CTA into the free lead magnet - A dedicated podcast-listener opt-in path on the hub - A short segmented welcome sequence that meets the listener already-warm and routes them toward the $997 course and GOAT Gang

Built on the hub and the email infrastructure from Layers 1 and 2.

Layer 5 · LinkedIn Distribution (Bottleneck #5, recurring engagement)

LinkedIn 3-4x/week, ghostwritten in Eric's voice from his existing operator knowledge, see content-strategy for the full content plan. Routes back into the hub. Ongoing monthly engagement.

Phase Mapping

Phase 1 · Install · Days 1-7 (one-time setup) - Hub deployed and live (responsive, brand-led) - Free lead magnet built and email-gated - Email infrastructure + the 5-email nurture flow live - Legible ladder section live on the hub and landing page - A5 wholesaling compliance disclaimer in the landing footer - IG bio link, YouTube link, LinkedIn profile link re-pointed to the hub

Phase 2 · Install · Days 7-21 - Podcast funnel live (show-notes CTA, listener opt-in path, segmented welcome sequence) - Podcast show notes updated across the back catalogue where practical - VSL recorded once brand colors and final framing are locked

Phase 3 · Recurring Engagement · Week 2+ · Monthly - LinkedIn 3-4x/week ghostwriting begins - Routes back into the hub; the email list grows from LinkedIn-sourced opt-ins

Phase 4 · Post-Install · Year-2 Layers - Brand-surface consolidation (retire the duplicate Facebook page, clarify where Run It Remote and Prime Partner sit relative to the coaching ladder) - Paid traffic activated once the connected funnel is proven - Hero VSL expanded beyond the preview if the brand benefits - DealCon promoted as a dedicated funnel once the hub is the established front door

Tech Stack (proposed, confirmed Day 1)

  • Hub + landing: static HTML deploy (fast, durable), or Eric's existing page builder if he prefers, confirm Day 1
  • Email: Eric's existing email platform; if none is set up for list-building, a standard ESP wired on Day 1
  • Lead magnet delivery: email-gated, delivered via the welcome sequence
  • Community: GOAT Gang stays on Skool, unchanged
  • Course: the $997 course stays on its existing platform, unchanged
  • Analytics: lightweight page analytics on the hub

What Does NOT Change

  • The $997 course, its price, its platform, its content
  • GOAT Gang on Skool
  • ELITE as the premium tier
  • DealCon as the event
  • Run It Remote as a separate company (not folded into the coaching ladder)
  • Eric's voice, brand, and positioning, "King of Virtual Wholesale RE" stays exactly as is

The install adds the connective layer. It does not touch the offers.
