Phase 1

VSL Preview Script · Eric Cline

Prepared 2026-05-13 by AG1 Consulting. A short 60-90 second preview script for the hub and landing page video poster, NOT a full long-form VSL. It records on Day 1 of the install once brand colors and final framing are locked. The poster stub is in place on both pages to reserve the asset slot. Written in Eric's voice, see copy-strategy. The AG1 universal banned-phrase list applies.

Purpose

This is the ~60-90 second preview that sits behind the play button on the hub VSL section and the landing page hero video. It is not the sales asset, it is the "here is what this is" preview that gives a scrolling visitor the verbal version before they read the page. Per the D100 VSL standard, the preview is short by default; a full long-form VSL is a separate, later asset only if Eric wants it.

Run time

60-90 seconds. Tight. Eric's register is punchy, so the script is short by design, it should feel like the first 90 seconds of a closer's pitch, not a slow build.

The script

[On camera, Eric, direct to lens, full energy]

I closed over two hundred and fifty million dollars in real estate. Over the telephone. Never walked a property to do it.

Then I sold the operation and started teaching the system that built it.

Here's the thing I want you to see today. If you've been following me, you already know the course exists. You know GOAT Gang exists. You know there's elite coaching, the event, all of it.

What you've never had is one place that shows you the whole thing. Where it all sits. What's free, what's the course, what's the room, what's next.

That's what this page is. The free first-call script, the same one I used. The 5-step sales process. And the whole ladder, laid out, so you can see exactly where you fit and what your next move is.

Start with the free script. Work it this week. Then climb when you're ready.

Refuse to lose. Let's run it.

[End. Cut to hub / landing CTA.]

Poster design notes

The poster behind the play button (the static frame visible before click):

  • Eyebrow line: "The Virtual Wholesaling System · ~90 Seconds"
  • Poster headline (Cormorant italic, mirrors Eric's voice): "$250M closed on the phone. The whole system, one page."
  • Play button: brand-gold, centered
  • Source label, bottom corner: "Preview · 90 Sec"
  • Color treatment: dark base with brand-gold accent, matching the hub aesthetic. The landing page poster uses the lighter landing palette.

Click-handler routing

On the hub: clicking the poster reveals a note that the preview records on Day 1 of the install (stub in place). On the landing page: clicking the poster scrolls the visitor to the free lead magnet opt-in. Both behaviors are wired in the page JS already; the actual video embed swaps in on Day 1.

Notes for the install

  • Records once brand colors and final framing are locked with Eric on Day 1.
  • The script attributes the $250M figure to Eric speaking in first person, which is correct, it is his claim, said by him.
  • Keep the energy at Eric's baseline. If anything, the delivered version should be more aggressive than the script reads, not less. A measured, calm delivery would read off-brand.
  • "Refuse to lose" is Eric's own brand line; it is the natural sign-off and it is not banned slop.
  • The full long-form VSL is a separate, optional, later asset. This preview is the only video deliverable in the Year-1 core build.
